Welcome to the Internet Addiction Resource Center

Internet Addiction has become a growing issue in homes, schools, and workplaces. Many people find it difficult to establish a balance between healthy internet use and behaviors that negatively impact relationships, productivity, and general well-being as technology becomes more widespread in daily life.

This website was created as an internal HR resource to help employees:

  • Understand What Internet Addiction is
  • Recognize Common Signs and Symptoms
  • Build Healthy Digital Habits
  • Access Support and Ways to Seek Help

This website offers easy-to-access information to help you maintain balance and productivity, whether you’re worried about your own internet use or want to help a friend.
