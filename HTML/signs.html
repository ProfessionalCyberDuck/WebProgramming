Signs, Symptoms, and Risk Factors

Internet addiction affects behavior, emotions, and physical health. Recognizing early signs can help prevent long-term impacts.

Behavioral Signs

  • Spending more time online than intended
  • Difficulty reducing internet use
  • Ignoring responsibilities at school or work
  • Losing track of time while online

Emotional Signs

  • Feeling anxious or restless when offline
  • Guilt about internet usage
  • Using the internet to escape stress
  • Feeling empty without digital activity

Physical Signs

  • Headaches or eye strain
  • Poor sleep habits
  • Fatigue from late-night use
  • Reduced physical activity

Risk Factors

  • High stress or burnout
  • Social isolation
  • Easy access to devices
  • Jobs requiring heavy internet use
  • Depression or anxiety
