Signs, Symptoms, and Risk Factors

Common Signs and Symptoms

Internet addiction can develop physically, emotionally, and behaviorally. Early detection of these symptoms can help avoid more serious issues.

Behavioral Signs

  • Spending more time online than planned
  • Trouble reducing internet usage
  • Ignoring school, work, or responsibilities
  • Losing track of time when using the internet

Emotional Signs

  • Being agitated or nervous when not online
  • Feeling guilty about how much time you spent online
  • Using the internet as a distraction or source of comfort
  • Feeling bored or “empty” without digital stimulation

Physical Signs

  • Headaches or eye strain
  • Poor sleep habits
  • Exhaustion from late-night internet use
  • Reduced exercise

Risk Factors

  • Excessive stress or burnout
  • Social isolation
  • Easy access to electronic devices
  • Jobs involving a lot of internet activity
  • Depression or underlying anxiety
