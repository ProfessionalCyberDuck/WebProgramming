Prevention, Healthy Habits, and Support

Building awareness and small daily habits can help maintain a healthy balance with technology.

Healthy Habits

  • Set daily limits for non-work internet use
  • Take regular breaks from screens
  • Turn off non-essential notifications
  • Keep devices out of reach at night

Workplace Tips

  • Use tools to stay focused
  • Avoid too many tabs at once
  • Practice mindful technology use
  • Set boundaries for after-hours communication

Support Options

  • Talk to a supervisor or HR
  • Use Employee Assistance Programs (EAP)
  • Seek counseling or mental health support
  • Try digital wellness apps

Small, consistent changes can make a big difference in maintaining digital balance.
