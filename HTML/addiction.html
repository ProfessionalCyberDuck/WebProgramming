What Is Internet Addiction?

Internet addiction (Internet Addiction Disorder) is a pattern of excessive or poorly controlled online behavior that interferes with daily life, relationships, work, and mental health.

Common Types of Internet Addiction

  • Social Media Addiction – Constant scrolling and checking notifications
  • Online Gaming Addiction – Excessive time spent gaming
  • Streaming Addiction – Watching videos for long periods without stopping
  • Online Gambling – Compulsive betting behavior
  • Online Shopping Addiction – Impulsive browsing and purchasing

Anyone can develop internet addiction regardless of age or background. It often builds gradually and may go unnoticed until it begins affecting daily life.
