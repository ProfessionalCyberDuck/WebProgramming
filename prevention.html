Preventions, Healthy Habits, and Support

Building Healthy Digital Habits

With awareness and intentional habits, it is possible to use the internet in a healthy way. These techniques can help in establishing balance.

Healthy Habits

  • Create daily time limits for non-work internet use
  • Take regular pauses from using screens
  • Disable notifications that are not important
  • Keep devices out of sight at night

Workplace Tips

  • Use productivity tools to stay focused
  • Refrain from using several tabs at once
  • Practice mindful technology use
  • Set limits for after-hour communication

Support Options

  • Consult a supervisor or HR
  • Use Employee Assistance Programs (EAP)
  • Seek mental health assistance or counseling
  • Consider time-management or digital wellness apps

Digital balance can be significantly improved by just taking small steps.
